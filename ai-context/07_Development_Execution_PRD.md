2\. Development Philosophy
==========================

Rule 1
------

Build MVP only.

Do not build:

*   AI features
    
*   Automation builder
    
*   CRM
    
*   Team management
    
*   Billing
    

Rule 2
------

Ship fast.

Focus on:

*   SMTP
    
*   Contacts
    
*   Templates
    
*   Campaigns
    
*   Email delivery
    

Rule 3
------

Every feature must be production-ready before moving forward.
